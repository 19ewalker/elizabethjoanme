Learn More About Me

Hi, I'm Elizabeth. I'm a sophomore at Columbia University in the City of New York, majoring in Computer Science. I'm from Mechanicsburg, Pennsylvania, and I graduated from Cumberland Valley High School in 2019.

On campus, I was the assistant music director of Columbia's production of The Last Five Years produced by the Columbia Musical Theater Society. I'm also a member of Women in Computer Science Columbia.

I wasn't always a Columbia student though! I am a transfer from Cornell where I was a sponsorship coordinator for Cornell's largest student-run hackathon, BigRed//Hacks, and I was a mentee in the Women in Computing program.

My hobbies include playing classical piano, reading bullet journaling, and playing tabletop games with friends. I also love taking long walks around Manhattan, which can range from 8-12 miles at a time.
